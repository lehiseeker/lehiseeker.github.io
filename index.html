Getting Started

December 18, 2019

Well, I'm starting on this new adventure of trying to learn to be a developer. Of course, I'm filled with self-doubts as to whether I will really ever pick this up. I've been doing exercises that teach me small concepts that seem so far removed from ever being able to do anything substantive. Also, I do an exercise, but the concept gets stuck in my short-term memory, but when I go back, I often can't remember. Also, since there are so many things to learn, I don't know what is important to memorize, and what I don't need to memorize. I'm telling myself that I just need to stick with it, and not to let all my doubts keep me from learning this.

Trying to Do a Little Bit Daily

December 19, 2019
Trying to balance life: kids, work, marriage, chores, and a little bit of downtime, is tough work to start with. So how can I actually learn to be a developer in the midst of it. The reality is, while I have a full-time job, it will be difficult. I think the path forward for me is to do a little bit every single day, even if it is only for a few minutes. I think the way my memory and brain work is that I need to reinforce it often.
